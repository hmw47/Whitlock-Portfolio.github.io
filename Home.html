The art I create is the way I can communicate with the world. With the help of different media and techniques, I can express a variety of emotions and views without using a single word. While other ways may fall short, art tends to allow me to portray my thoughts into tangible and comprehensible objects. The introduction of digital media into my practice pushed my personal boundaries by using a medium that seemed impressionistic and impalpable to express myself and connect with those around me. My objective this semester was to push my limits and expand my knowledge of the digital world. Through digital media, I learned that there are endless opportunities to create and connect.

My portfolio is a variety of experimental processes and styles. The body of my work explores the worth of different approaches to composing a digital work of art and embraces the imperfections of novicity. Ultimately, I intend to grow and familiarize myself with the language of digital content through creating and sharing art.
